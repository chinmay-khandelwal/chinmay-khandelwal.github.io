About Chinmay

Experience

Chinmay Khandelwal is a data scientist with expertise across Healthcare, Finance, E-commerce, and Marketing. 

Currently, Chinmay is a Sr. Data Scientist at Deloitte, where they drive data-driven solutions that enhance decision-making and operational efficiency. Before joining Deloitte, Chinmay was the first hire in the data department of an e-commerce startup, building the foundation of the data team and establishing essential analytics practices to scale the business. Earlier in their career, Chinmay served as a Research Assistant at the AIR Lab for Secure Systems at the University of South Florida, contributing to advancements in secure system technologies. 

In addition to a broad industry experience, Chinmay holds a Master’s in Industrial Engineering from the University of South Florida, bringing a solid technical background and a passion for solving complex challenges to their role. 

Outside of work, Chinmay actively contributes to the data science community, sharing insights on data strategies, analytics tools, and the impact of data on business innovation.
